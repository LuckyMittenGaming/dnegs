A-Z Build Guardrails

The build should be beautiful, but also impossible to miss the basics.

The attached checklist becomes our friendly internal operating system: strategy, IA, UX, visual design, development, content, QA, launch, and post-launch readiness.

01

Strategy + IA

Sitemap, navigation model, content inventory, feature list, legal/compliance checks, and redirect planning.

02

Visual System

Dark cinematic palette, typography scale, motion language, accessibility contrast, and responsive behavior specs.

03

Interactive Build

Semantic HTML, custom CSS, resilient JavaScript, reduced-motion fallback, keyboard support, and mobile-first layout.

04

QA + Launch

Broken-link checks, form tests, browser/device QA, Lighthouse, accessibility audit, security, backups, and launch smoke test.
